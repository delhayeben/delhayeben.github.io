About me

I am currently a Post-Doctoral scholar at the Bensmaia Lab at the University of Chicago.

I graduated as a Electro-mechanical engineer from Université catholique de Louvain (BS 2007, MS 2009) and I received a PhD in applied science (2014) from the same university for my work entitled "Skin mechanics involved in dynamic touch", this work was co-supervised by Jean-Louis Thonnard and Phillipe Lefèvre Phillipe Lefèvre.

My research interests include neuroprosthetics, haptics and neuroscience in the context of touch and sensory-motor control.

During my free time, I like running (see Running), my favorite distances are half-marathon (1:23:40) and marathon (3:08:52).
